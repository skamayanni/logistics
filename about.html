Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our shipping services, delivery times, package tracking, and more.

How can I track my shipment?

Once your shipment has been processed, you'll receive a tracking number via email or SMS. Enter the tracking number on our website to view real-time updates from pickup to delivery.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on the destination and shipping service selected. Most domestic shipments arrive within 1–5 business days, while international deliveries typically take 5–10 business days.

What items can I ship with SwiftShip?

We ship documents, parcels, retail orders, business freight, and bulk cargo. Certain hazardous or restricted items may not be accepted, so please review our shipping guidelines before booking.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes. SwiftShip provides reliable international shipping services with customs assistance, secure handling, and real-time tracking to destinations around the world.

What happens if my package is delayed?

While we strive to deliver every shipment on time, unexpected weather or customs delays can occasionally occur. Our support team will keep you informed and assist you until your package is delivered.
